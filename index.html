Training Program

Day 1: IPv6 Foundations

IPv6 introduction, addressing schemes, and OSPFv3 theory with hands-on laboratory exercises

Day 2: Advanced Routing

BGP theory and comprehensive labs covering iBGP, eBGP, Route Reflectors, and peer groups, plus IPv6 deployment planning

Day 3: Real-world Applications

IPv6 in WLAN environments, security configurations, server setup, troubleshooting techniques, and certification ceremony

Key Achievements

Technical Excellence

Comprehensive training on IPv6 deployment covering network planning, dual-stack operations, advanced routing protocols (OSPFv3, BGP), and security best practices.

Hands-on Experience

Practical laboratory sessions with real-world scenarios, enabling participants to gain confidence in IPv6 deployment across various network environments.

Knowledge Transfer

All 29 participants received official certificates and comprehensive lab materials, empowering them to serve as IPv6 advocates in their home institutions.

Regional Collaboration

Strengthened networking relationships between Asian NRENs, fostering ongoing collaboration in IPv6 deployment initiatives.
